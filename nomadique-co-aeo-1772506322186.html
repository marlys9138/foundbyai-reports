Schema Markup & Brand JSON
The machine-readable layer — invisible to visitors but essential for AI. Tells search engines and AI crawlers exactly how to classify your brand, products, and FAQs.
WHAT TO DO WITH THIS
1
Copy the JSON-LD block and give it to your developer to paste inside the <head> tag of your homepage.
2
For the brand-facts.json file — ask your developer to upload it to /.well-known/brand-facts.json on your server.
3
Test your schema after it's live at validator.schema.org — paste your homepage URL and check for errors.
4
No developer? Most platforms (Shopify, Squarespace, Webflow) have a "custom code" section in settings where you can paste JSON-LD directly.
